# Empório LeleCute - Product Overview

## What is this?

Empório LeleCute is a Brazilian e-commerce platform specializing in artisanal products, particularly in the categories of soaps (sabonetes), candles (velas), gift items (lembrancinhas), and gift kits. The platform features a modern, feminine design with a coral/salmon color scheme that reflects the brand's identity.

## Key Features

### Public-Facing Features
- **Product Catalog**: Browse products by category with filtering and search
- **Product Pages**: Detailed product information with image galleries
- **Shopping Cart**: Persistent cart stored in localStorage with quantity management
- **Checkout Flow**: Multi-step checkout including shipping information
- **Order Tracking**: Track orders with tracking codes
- **Landing Pages**: Specialized landing pages for occasions (baby showers, weddings, birthdays, etc.)
- **Blog**: Content management for blog posts
- **FAQ & Testimonials**: Customer support sections
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Floating WhatsApp button for customer support

### Admin Features
- **Product Management**: Create, edit, import products with WYSIWYG editor
- **Category & Tag Management**: Organize products with custom categories and tags
- **Order Management**: View and manage customer orders
- **Customer Management**: View customer information and order history
- **SEO Management**: Full control over meta tags, sitemaps, and robots.txt
- **Instagram Integration**: Schedule and sync Instagram posts
- **Content Management**: Edit homepage blocks, hero slides, testimonials
- **User Management**: Admin access control and audit logging
- **Tracking & Analytics**: Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel integration

## Target Audience

- Brazilian consumers (PT-BR language)
- Customers looking for artisanal gifts and specialty products
- People shopping for special occasions (baby showers, weddings, birthdays)
- Businesses looking for custom gift options

## Business Model

- Direct-to-consumer e-commerce
- Custom product personalization options
- Occasion-specific marketing and landing pages
- Instagram-driven social commerce
